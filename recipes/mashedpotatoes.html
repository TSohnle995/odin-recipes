Slow Cooker Mashed Potatoes

Fast and easy, 7 ingredient slow-cooker Mashed Potatoes!

Preparation

  1. Place potatoes, butter, salt, pepper, and water in a large slow cooker.
  2. Cover and cook on high for 4 hours.
  3. Remove lid and mash the potatoes with a masher or beaters, mixing in the milk.
  4. Stir in chives
  5. Stir in chives
  6. Keep the slow cooker on "warm"
  7. Enjoy!

Ingredients

  • 4 lb potatoes, peeled and diced
  • 1 1/2 cups butter
  • 1 tbsp salt
  • 1 teaspoon pepper
  • 1 cup water
  • 1 1/2 cups milk, warm
  • 2 tablespoons chives, optional
