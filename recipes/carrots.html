Thyme and Rosemary Carrots

Flavorful rosemary carrots go with just about any entree. If you don't have fresh herbs on hand, feel free to use dried.

Directions

  1. Prepare carrots according to package directions. Meanwhile, combine remaining ingredients. Drain carrots; top with herb butter.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups frozen sliced carrots
  • 1/4 cup butter, softened
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh thyme
  • 1/2 teaspoon minced fresh rosemary
  • 1/8 teaspoon salt
